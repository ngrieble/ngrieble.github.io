Description

My team decided to make a Web App that does interesting data mining on the users Facebook photos. More specifically, our program will be a site that the user enters their Facebook credentials upon entry, then it will load a suite of graphs and charts, displaying information about their pictures on Facebook. The graphs will be similar to but not limited by; "top likers" on photos you've uploaded, "top commenters" on photos you're tagged in, top location of photos, ect.

The flow of the site will be a modern site where you scroll down to get to the next "page" and the site is one continuous page, still with shortcuts to different locations. It will also be responsive, enabling use on mobile phones and tablets. From each graph, the user can click on part of it to expand the site into details about their facebook friends or specific photo.
